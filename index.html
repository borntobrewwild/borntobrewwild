Beer Sourness List

List of sour beers pH and titratable acidity


As the sour beer becomes more popular, therefore more expensive, in Czech republic, I sadly found some breweries mislabeling non-sour beer as sour beers to make a little extra money. This drove me to start monitoring the sourness of the craft beer scene in Czech republic and around. More information on my blog Born to Brew Wild. If you want to send in a beer sample, contact me.
